About Us

Why Choose Us?

PT. Zeus Prima Garda a lightning protection company established in Indonesia as a country under the equator with the highest thunderstormdays in the world according to Guiness Book of Record. Backup with the most experience experts in this field and support by the Lightning Research Centre in Bandung West Java. The company produces the most suitable ESE Air Terminal Lightning protection, Electrostatic Air Terminal and surge arrester for application in the tropical and sub-tropical area.

Our Business

PT. Zeus Prima Garda manufactures: surge arrester & Air Terminal ESE, Collection Volume Technology , Electrostatic Lightning Protection and the Conventional Air Terminal to provide safety for the assets of the user against surge & lightning hazard.

And since 1985 , the company has organized a course (training) on Surge-Lightning Protection System and up to now the training have been participated by many engineers and technicians represented the companies such as oil & gas, telecommunication company, satellite & cellular provider, mining company, power plant, manufacture and many others
